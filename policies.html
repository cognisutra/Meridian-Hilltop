HOTEL POLICIES

Rules & Regulations

Please review our policies before your stay.

Check-in & Check-out

  • Check-in time: 12:00 Noon
  • Check-out time: 10:00 AM
  • Early check-in subject to availability (extra charges apply)
  • Late check-out subject to availability (extra charges apply)

Payment Terms

  • Advance 50% upon confirmation — non-refundable
  • Balance 50% payable during check-in
  • Payments accepted: Cash, UPI (GPay/PhonePe), Bank Transfer (NEFT/IMPS), Paytm

Meal Plans

  • CP Plan — Staying with Breakfast
  • MAP Plan — Stay + Breakfast + Lunch OR Dinner per person/day
  • AP Plan — Stay + Breakfast + Lunch + Dinner per person/day

House Rules

  • Free Wi-Fi as per available network strength in common areas
  • Common campfire with music included
  • Indoor games: Billiards, Carrom, Table Tennis, Chess
  • Smart television with cable in all rooms
  • 24-hour centralized hot water
  • Swimming pool: 8 AM to 6 PM. Synthetic wear mandatory
  • Multi-Cuisine restaurant: 8 AM to 9:30 PM
  • All rooms are non-smoking
  • Pets are not allowed
  • Outside food is not allowed. Additional corkage & cleaning charges apply

Additional Info

  • Extra bed available at ₹500/night
  • Candle light dinner available on request at ₹1,500
  • Airport/Railway pickup and drop available (charges apply)
  • Bonfire and indoor games are part of complimentary amenities
