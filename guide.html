Attractions Near Meridian Hilltop

Our resort is ideally located close to Yercaud's finest spots.

2.5 km from resort

Shevaroyan Temple

Located on the way from our resort, the Shevaroyan Temple is a historic hilltop shrine dedicated to Lord Shevaroyan. The temple offers panoramic views of the surrounding hills and is a must-visit for its spiritual significance and scenic location.

3 km from resort

Lady's Seat

A popular viewpoint offering breathtaking views of the Salem plains and the Ghats. The natural rock formation shaped like a seat is perfect for photography and watching the sunset.

4 km from resort

Yercaud Lake

The serene Yercaud Lake is the heart of the town. Enjoy boating, take a stroll along the lakefront, or simply relax at the adjacent park. The lake is surrounded by lush gardens and is a favorite spot for families.

4.5 km from resort

Botanical Garden

The Government Botanical Garden in Yercaud is home to a wide variety of exotic plants, flowers, and trees. A peaceful retreat for nature lovers, it also features a small children's play area.

5 km from resort

Kiliyur Falls

A spectacular waterfall cascading from a height of 300 feet, Kiliyur Falls is a short trek from the main road. The trail through the forest is an adventure in itself, especially during the monsoon season.

6 km from resort

Pagoda Point

Named after the distinctive pagoda-shaped rock formations, this viewpoint offers sweeping views of the Salem city below. An ideal spot for sunrise and photography enthusiasts.

50.9 km from resort

Salem Airport

The nearest airport is Salem Airport (SVX), well-connected to Bangalore, Chennai, and Hyderabad. Our resort offers airport pickup and drop services at nominal charges.

32 km from resort

Salem Railway Station

The nearest major railway station is Salem Town Junction, well-connected to all major Indian cities. Pickup and drop services are available from the resort.
